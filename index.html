About Us

Janaki Global is a family-founded enterprise committed to providing high-quality organic millets, moringa, spices, pulses, and herbal teas to customers worldwide.

Founded by Sandeep Abojwar, Neha Abojwar, Saurabh Abojwar, and Gauri Abojwar, we leverage decades of experience in agriculture and export to ensure quality and sustainability.
