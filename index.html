About Janaki Global

Janaki Global is committed to bringing the finest organic and natural food products from India to the world. Our mission is to support sustainable farming while delivering premium quality millets, moringa, spices, and other agro-products to global markets.

  • Organic Millets (Pearl, Foxtail, Finger)
  • Moringa Leaf Powder & Capsules
  • Indian Spices (Turmeric, Cumin, Coriander, etc.)
  • Herbal Teas and Powders
  • Specialty Rice Varieties
