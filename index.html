About Janaki Global

Janaki Global is a family-founded company committed to bringing authentic and high-quality Indian natural and organic food products to the global market. Founded by Sandeep, Neha, Saurabh, and Gauri Abojwar, we specialize in millets, moringa, spices, and more.
